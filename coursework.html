Coursework

For engineering couse under KTU university we had 8 semesters and we studied almost 58 subjects and have 180 credits and 2 extra credits for extra curricular activities.

These 58 subjects can be classified into four categories:

  • core subjects in CS
  • Mathematics
  • Hot topics in Computer Science
  • Business and Management Subjects

Core subjects in CS

They are :

  • COMPUTER ORGANIZATION AND ARCHITECTURE
  • OPERATING SYSTEMS
  • OBJECT ORIENTED DESIGN AND PROGRAMMING
  • PRINCIPLES OF DATABASE DESIGN
  • THEORY OF COMPUTATION
  • SYSTEM SOFTWARE
  • DESIGN AND ANALYSIS OF ALGORITHMS
  • COMPILER DESIGN
  • COMPUTER NETWORKS

Mathematics

This includes:

  • CALCULUS
  • LINEAR ALGEBRA & COMPLEX ANALYSIS
  • PROBABILITY DISTRIBUTIONS,TRANSFORMS AND NUMERICAL METHODS
  • GRAPH THEORY AND COMBINATORICS
  • DISCRETE COMPUTATIONAL STRUCTURES

Hot topics in Computer Science

These are mainly Electives ,one can opt according to their interst.They are:

  • SOFT COMPUTING
  • DIGITAL IMAGE PROCESSING
  • CRYPTOGRAPHY AND NETWORK SECURITY
  • DATA MINING AND WARE HOUSING

Business and Management Subjects

  • BUSINESS ECONOMICS
  • PRINCIPLES OF MANAGEMENT
  • SOFTWARE ENGINEERING AND PROJECT MANAGEMENT
